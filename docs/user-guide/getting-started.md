# User Guide: Getting Started

The K-YEY platform is a high-performance logistics dashboard designed for ease of use.

## Login & Access

- Use your **Username** or **Phone Number** with your registered password.
- If you have Two-Factor Auth enabled (check `AuthController.php` logic), follow the prompts.

## Interface Navigation

- **Primary Sidebar**: Access modules (Orders, Financials, CRM, Settings).
- **Navbar Search**: Use the global search at the top for quick actions.
- **Role Isolation**: Your screen will look different based on your role (Admin vs Client).

## First Steps

### 1. Configure Governorates & Cities

If you are an Admin, you MUST first set up the geographic areas in the **Management** section. This is crucial for calculating delivery fees.

### 2. Define Shipping Plans

Check the **Plans** module. All Clients must be assigned a shipping plan to define the rates and terms of their orders.

### 3. Add Clients & Shippers

In the **Users & CRM** section, create the business partners who will be using the system.
