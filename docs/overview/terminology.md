# Glossary of Terms

To ensure consistent communication, the project uses these specific terms:

| Term | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| **Shipper** | A delivery representative or carrier (Staff User) responsible for physical shipment delivery. |
| **Client** | A business or individual who sends the orders through our platform. |
| **Order** | A single delivery shipment from Client to Receiver. |
| **Governorate** | A primary geographic area/region (e.g. Cairo, Alexandria). |
| **City** | A specific district or area within a Governorate. |
| **Shipper Collection** | The process where a Shipper hands over collected COD money to the company. |
| **Client Settlement** | The process where the company pays the Client for their delivered orders after fees. |
| **COD** | Cash on Delivery: The total amount collected from the final receiver. |
| **RBAC** | Role-Based Access Control: The permission system (Admin, Shipper, Client, etc.). |
| **Cubit** | The state management pattern used in the Flutter mobile application. |
