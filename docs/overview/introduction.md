# Project Introduction & Overview

**K-YEY Logistics** is a high-performance delivery management platform designed for delivery companies to efficiently manage their operations, from order ingestion to final financial reconciliation.

## Business Objectives

The platform aims to:

- **Streamline Operations**: Automate order assignment and tracking for representatives (shippers).
- **Financial Integrity**: Ensure accurate collection of COD (Cash on Delivery) and timely settlements for clients.
- **Data Transparency**: Provide real-time statistics and activity logs for all stakeholders.
- **Mobile Empowerment**: Give representatives a dedicated mobile tool for scanning, status updates, and performance tracking.

## Core Modules

- **Order Management**: Life-cycle tracking of shipments (Out for Delivery, Delivered, Undelivered, etc.).
- **Logistics Operations**: Managing pickups, visits, and material requests.
- **Financial Reconciliation**: Shipper collections and Client settlements.
- **User CRM**: Managing Clients, Shippers, and Administrative Staff.
- **System Configuration**: Pricing plans, area management (Governorates/Cities), and business rules.

## Stakeholders & Roles

1. **Super Admin**: Full system access, bypasses all permissions.
2. **Admin**: Operational management of the entire platform.
3. **Shipper (Representative)**: Uses the mobile app to handle deliveries and collections.
4. **Client**: Uses the dashboard to track their orders and settlements.
5. **Follower**: Read-only access for monitoring purposes.

## Key Workflows

1. **Order Creation**: Client or Admin creates an order.
2. **Assignment**: Order is assigned to a Shipper.
3. **Delivery**: Shipper updates status (delivered/undelivered) via the mobile app.
4. **Collection**: Shipper returns collected money to the company (Shipper Collection).
5. **Settlement**: Company pays the Client for delivered orders (Client Settlement).
